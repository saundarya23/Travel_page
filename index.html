Travel Agency

Travel agencies can also serve as general sales agents for airlines
that do not have offices in a specific region.
A travel agency's main function is to act as an agent,
selling travel products and services on behalf of a supplier.
They are also called Travel Advisors. They do not keep inventory in-hand unless
they have pre-booked hotel rooms or cabins on a cruise ship for a group travel event
such as a wedding, honeymoon, or other group event.
